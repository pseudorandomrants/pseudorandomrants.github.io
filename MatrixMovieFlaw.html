Fixing the Flaw in Matrix Movie

Machines using humans as power source is fundamentally flawed -

  1. There is no perpetual motion machine (of 1st or 2nd kind), feeding dead humans intravenously to living humans will eventually run out.
  2. Machines could have used other lower forms of animals as power source. They would accept any simulation easily, unlike complicated human brain.
So what's the alternative story? Well, machines enslaved humans and used them as supercomputers. To run their models etc. (like humans use supercomputers today).
